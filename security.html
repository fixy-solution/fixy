Security

We take your security seriously. Here's how we protect you

1. Commitment to Security

Fixy Solution is committed to maintaining the highest standards of data security and protection. We employ multiple layers of security measures to protect your personal information, payment details, and service data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

2. Data Encryption

All sensitive information transmitted through our website and mobile applications is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption protocols. This ensures that your data is protected during transmission between your device and our servers.

3. Professional Vetting & Background Checks

All professionals on our platform undergo rigorous vetting processes, including:

  • Comprehensive background checks
  • License and insurance verification
  • Reference verification
  • Criminal record screening
  • Ongoing compliance monitoring

4. Secure Payment Processing

We partner with PCI-DSS compliant payment processors to handle all financial transactions. We never store complete credit card information on our servers. All payment processing is handled through secure, encrypted channels.

5. Account Security

We recommend the following to protect your account:

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Do not share your login credentials
  • Log out of shared devices
  • Report suspicious activity immediately

6. Database Security

Our databases are protected by:

  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Encrypted backups
  • Redundancy and disaster recovery protocols

7. Staff Training

All Fixy Solution employees with access to personal data receive regular security training, including data protection, secure handling practices, and privacy regulations compliance.

8. Incident Response

In the event of a security breach, Fixy Solution has established procedures to:

  • Immediately investigate and contain the incident
  • Notify affected users without unreasonable delay
  • Cooperate with law enforcement as required
  • Document and learn from the incident
  • Implement corrective measures

9. Third-Party Security

We carefully vet all third-party service providers and partners to ensure they maintain comparable security standards. We require contractual commitments to data protection and conduct regular security assessments.

10. Compliance Standards

Fixy Solution complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)
  • HIPAA (where applicable)
  • Industry best practices and standards

11. Regular Security Testing

We conduct regular security assessments including:

  • Vulnerability scanning
  • Penetration testing
  • Security audits
  • Code reviews
  • Compliance checks

12. Insurance Coverage

All professionals on our platform are required to carry appropriate liability insurance. Fixy Solution maintains comprehensive insurance coverage to protect both customers and service professionals.

13. Reporting Security Concerns

If you discover a security vulnerability or have concerns about your account security, please report it immediately to security@fixysolution.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will address all reports promptly and confidentially.

14. Regular Updates

We continuously update and improve our security measures to address emerging threats and protect against new vulnerabilities. Your data security is an ongoing priority.

15. Contact Us

For security questions or concerns, please contact us at security@fixysolution.com or visit our contact page.
