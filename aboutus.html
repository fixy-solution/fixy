Our Story

How Fixy Solution started

Fixy Solution was founded on a simple observation: finding a reliable home repair professional shouldn't be stressful. We've all experienced the frustration of searching for a trustworthy plumber, electrician, or handyman - making phone calls, waiting on hold, getting inconsistent quotes, and hoping we've made the right choice.

Our founders experienced this pain firsthand. After spending weeks trying to find reliable professionals for various home repairs, they realized there had to be a better way. What if there was a single platform where homeowners could easily find vetted, professional workers? What if booking a repair was as simple as ordering food online?

That vision led to the creation of Fixy Solution. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers and support hundreds of skilled professionals in their communities. We're just getting started, and we're excited about the future of home repairs.
