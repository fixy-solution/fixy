Frequently Asked Questions

⏱️ How quickly can I get a professional?

Our available professionals typically start as early as the same day or next day, depending on their schedules and your location. You can filter by availability when booking to find someone who works with your schedule.

🔐 Are the professionals insured and verified?

Absolutely! All professionals in our network are fully licensed, insured, and background-checked. We verify every credential for your complete peace of mind.

💯 What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the service, we'll work to make it right or provide a full refund. Your satisfaction is our priority.

💳 How do I pay for services?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets. Payments are secure and processed through encrypted systems. You can pay through our app or website.

📅 Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes! You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment without any penalties or additional charges. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.

👤 What if the professional doesn't show up?

If a professional fails to arrive, we'll immediately find you a replacement or provide a full refund. Your satisfaction is our priority and we take no-shows very seriously.
